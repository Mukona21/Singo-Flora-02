ABOUT SINGOFLORA PTY(LTD)

Singo Flora: Bringing Beauty to Pretoria for Over 20 Years
Nestled in the heart of Pretoria, Singo Flora isn't just a flower farm, it's a family legacy blooming for over two decades. We're more than just rows of vibrant blooms; we're a passionate team weaving stories with every stem, every petal, every carefully crafted arrangement.
Our journey began with a simple dream, nurtured by sun-kissed skin and calloused hands. We sowed seeds of hope, love, and dedication, watching them blossom into a vibrant tapestry of colors, textures, and intoxicating scents. Today, our fields are alive with a symphony of roses blushing crimson, lilies reaching for the sky, and gerberas ablaze with fiery hues. Each bloom whispers a silent tale, a testament to the unwavering rhythm of nature's heart.
But Singo Flora is more than a feast for the senses. It's a haven for city souls seeking solace in the quiet hum of bees and the gentle sway of wildflowers. It's an escape for dreamers who find inspiration in the unfolding petals and a canvas for artists who capture nature's fleeting beauty. We invite you to step into our world, where worries fade like morning mist and anxieties melt away with every inhaled breath of fresh, flower-scented air.
Whether you seek a single, perfect rose to whisper sweet nothings, or a breathtaking bouquet that speaks volumes, our hands, guided by generations of experience, will craft something extraordinary. We offer fresh blooms bursting with life, delicate dried arrangements that whisper memories of summer days, and bespoke creations that capture the essence of your heart's desire.
Come, wander our fields, feel the velvety petals beneath your fingertips, and witness the silent magic of life unfolding before your eyes. Singo Flora awaits, ready to bloom in your home, at your celebration, or simply in your quiet moments of reflection. Let us paint your world with nature's vibrant melody, one bloom at a time.
More than flowers, we're family. More than a farm, we're a legacy. More than beauty, we're Singo Flora.

IS HOW SINGO FLORA EMPOWERS YOUR BUSINESS

  • 1

    Custom Seedling & Cutting Propagation:

    Tired of limited options? Singo Flora offers a solution. We specialize in propagating seedlings and cuttings of unique and in-demand flowers tailored to your specific needs. This allows you to offer a wider variety of blooms to your customers without the hassle of starting from scratch.

  • 2

    Expert Conditioning & Processing:

    Fresh flowers deserve the best treatment. Our team provides expert conditioning and processing services to ensure your blooms arrive in peak condition. This includes hydration, temperature control, precise stem trimming, and anti-ethylene treatment – all designed to maximize the lifespan and beauty of your flowers.

  • 3

    Unparalleled Grower Expertise:

    Years of experience cultivating exquisite flowers have instilled in us a wealth of knowledge. We offer consultation services to help you select the perfect flower varieties for your climate, optimize growing conditions in your shop, or troubleshoot any pest or disease issues you might encounter.
